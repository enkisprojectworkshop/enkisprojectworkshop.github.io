Collaborative Workshop
Shaping Responsible AI Education for All

The workshop is part of the BMBF-funded project ENKIS. The aim of the ENKIS project is to establish sustainable courses for responsible AI at Freie Universität Berlin for three different audiences: computer science students, non-computer science students, and student teachers of all school subjects.

However, since the audiences are interdisciplinary and the AI topic is broad and increasingly complex, each of these AI courses presents different needs. Therefore, a joint workforce is needed in order to develop these sustainable courses effectively. Consequently, the workshop is one of the paths to achieving this multidisciplinary collaboration for the development of the courses.

Considering that at Freie Universität Berlin and nearby universities, there are different professionals with different expertise regarding the AI topic, these are valuable actors to help in the effective development of these AI courses. Therefore, the objective of this workshop is to bring interested people together, give them opportunities to exchange their perspectives on AI (relevant topics, competencies) and to present and discuss the first research results of ENKIS.
